# Company-Analysis-Starbucks

Project Overview 

This project presents a comprehensive company and equity analysis of Starbucks Corporation (NASDAQ: SBUX), focusing on its business model, financial performance, competitive positioning, and intrinsic valuation. The analysis integrates financial statement evaluation, industry research, valuation modeling, and risk assessment to arrive at an investment recommendation.

- Historical financial performance (Revenue, EBITDA, Net Income, EPS)

- Liquidity, efficiency, and working capital analysis

- Cash flow analysis, capital expenditures, and capital allocation strategy

- Intrinsic valuation using Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) modeling

- Relative valuation against key competitors

- Investment risks, macroeconomic factors, and tariff impacts

Key Highlights

- Strong revenue growth and brand resilience post-pandemic

- Improving operational efficiency and inventory turnover

- Liquidity tightening reflected in declining current and quick ratios

- DCF valuation suggests the stock is overvalued relative to intrinsic value

- Premium valuation multiples compared to industry peers


Valuation & Recommendation

- Intrinsic Value (DCF): $70.36 per share

- Market Price (as of April 2025): ~$85

- Investment Recommendation: HOLD

- Strong brand equity and long-term growth prospects

- Valuation concerns and near-term liquidity pressures limit upside


Outcome

This project demonstrates the ability to perform end-to-end equity research, combine qualitative and quantitative analysis, build valuation models, and communicate investment insights in a structured and professional manner.
